# GymFlow — Membership Renewal & Follow-up Automation

**Stop Losing Members to Manual Follow-ups.**  
GymFlow automatically detects expiring memberships, sends timely reminders, and tracks renewals so you can increase retention with zero admin work.

---

### The Problem
Every month, gyms lose **5% to 15% of their members** simply because renewal follow-ups are manual, inconsistent, or forgotten. Owners and staff are too busy running the gym to text or email every expiring member, leading to missed revenue and high churn.

### The GymFlow Solution
GymFlow sits between your member directory (Google Sheets or Supabase) and your messaging channels (WhatsApp/Email) to run a 100% automated renewal campaign:

1. **Daily Auto-Scan**: Every morning, GymFlow scans your membership list for subscriptions expiring in **7 days**.
2. **Personalized Outreach**: It sends a friendly, automated WhatsApp or Email reminder to each member.
3. **Instant Reply Tracking**: 
   - If a member replies **YES**, GymFlow automatically flags them as **Interested/Renewed** in your directory.
   - If there is **no reply within 48 hours**, GymFlow automatically sends a gentle follow-up reminder.
4. **Owner Dashboard**: Gym owners see an updated view of active outreach, current renewal rates, and saved revenue.
